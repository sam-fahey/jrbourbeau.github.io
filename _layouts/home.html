About me

I'm a research assistant and physics Ph.D. candidate at University of Wisconsin - Madison. As a member of the IceCube Neutrino Observatory Collaboration, I conduct analyses searching for correlation of neutrino data with transient astrophysical objects.

My daily work consists mainly of software programming, statistics, and the communication of concepts therein with my peers. I'm interested in machine learning both for applications in my research and as a hobby.
